Floor Cleaner FAQs

Why Use Rootall..? 

Rootall product Formulated with strong & disinfectant Herbal Essential Oils, Which Protects from germs & viruses. it's strong fragrance keeps insects & mosquitoes away from your home, Providing Clean & Hyginic Environment.     

Benefits

* Non toxic and non corrosive
* It is completely baby safe and pet safe
* No phosphate, chlorine
* No ammonia
* No parabeans.    

Application Area

Vetrified, ceramic, marble, granite, mosaic, wooden floor.

Active Ingredients  

Tea Tree oil, Cinammon oil, Clove oil, Eucalyptus oil, Peppermint oil Camphor, Thymol, Citronella oil, Lemongrass oil, Aqua water, Strach based thikner, Herbal green colour.

Direction of use 

* Shake well before use
* Add 30ml in half bucket of water (aprox -3 ltr)
* Gently Mop.

Precaution 

For External use only , Keep out of reach children and pets, avoid contact with eyes,in case of contact rinse with water, Store away from direct sunlight.
Note: Do not mix with any other chemicals in rootall floor cleaner. Product contains natural ingredients, hence can vary in colour and fragrance and consistency, this does not affect product performance.
