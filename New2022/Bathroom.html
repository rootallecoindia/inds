Toilet Cleaner FAQs

Why Use Rootall..? 

Rootall product are made of powerfull natural ingrediants, for safty of you and your loved one, non toxic and non corrosive, free of ammonia and chlorine, eco friendly and biodegradable product.              

Benefits

* Safe for the user.
* Have zero toxic chemicals and protect against indoor pollution.
* Are made of natural plant based ingredients.
* Are eco friendly and biodegradable product.    

Application Area

Indian & western toilet bowl and urinals.              

Active Ingredients  

Aqua (water), organic corboxylic acid, Glaxical paste, Descalant, Vinegar, Lemon extract, thickner powder made of aloevera extract, colour, citrus fragreance.            

Direction of use 

* Shake well before use.
* Squirt the product arround the toilet bowl rim and surface.
* Spread equal proportion with help of brush and leave for 5 minute.
* Brush the toilet bowl * Flush rinse with water.              

Precaution 

For External use only , Keep out of reach children and pets, avoid contact with eyes, in case of contact rinse with water, Store away from direct sunlight.
Note: Do not mix with any other chemicals or acid in rootall toilet cleaner. Product contains natural ingredients, hence can vary in colour and fragrance and consistency, this does not affect product performance.
