Dishwash Liquid FAQs

Why Use Rootall..? 

"Rootall" is organic, Naturally derived ultra concentrated thick Dishwash Gel. Our unique Plant based formula is designed to clean & remove oil stains & grease from utensils. Does not contains any harmful chemicals.    

Safe & Effective Ingredients

Organic alpha foam, Aqua (water), plant derived cleaning agents (Quallaja saponaria), sodium thickner, cleaning enhancer (corn starch derived), essential oils & botanical extracts, Citrus aurantifolia (lime), citrus lemon Osbeck (nimbu), food colour, scrubbing powder. Biodegradable preservatives.     

Key Features

* With fresh and long-lasting Natural Citrus fragrance.
* Eco-friendly, healthy, you can use them safely.
* Totally safe for skin & eyes.
* Powerful grease removal.
* Softens hands when doing dishes.
* pH neutral, non toxic, Non-irritating.

Direction of use

* Shake well before use.
* Add one teaspoon in a bowl of water (approx 40ml),
* Gently scrub and rinse.
Note: Remove inner seal before use. (Use the sponge scrubber for best result).       

Precaution

For External use only, Keep out of reach children and pets, avoid contact with eyes, in case of contact rinse with water, Store away from direct sunlight.
Note: Do not mix with any other chemicals in rootall dishwash. Product contains natural ingredients, hence can vary in colour and fragrance and consistency and settle down, this does not affect product performance.
